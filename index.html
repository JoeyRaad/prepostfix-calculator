Prefix and Postfix Notations Guide

Prefix notation (Polish notation) places operators before operands, e.g., + 9 * 2 3 evaluates to + 9 6 then to 15.

Postfix notation (Reverse Polish notation) places operators after operands, e.g., 9 2 3 * + evaluates to 9 6 +then to 15.
